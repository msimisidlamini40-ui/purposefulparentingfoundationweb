The Problem

The Home Shouldn’t Be a Battleground

In a world that celebrates hustle, parents are stretched thin. Shouting, unresolved conflict, and emotional unavailability quietly distort a child’s sense of safety — not out of malice, but out of exhaustion and lack of tools.

“We don’t raise monsters on purpose. We raise them by accident when we fail to manage ourselves.”

Founder & Chairperson
Vusumzi Mthembu
Scriptwriting and Media · Author · Artisan
Who We Are

A South African non-profit company dedicated to redefining what it means to raise the next generation. Founded by Vusumzi Morris Mthembu — husband, father, author, and qualified artisan — our work is personal. It begins at home and ripples outward into communities across KwaZulu-Natal.

We don’t come with judgment. We come with tools, community, and a deep belief that every parent who shows up is capable of change.

Questions

Common Inquiries

Are the workshops free to attend?+

Our community support circles are always free. Some intensive workshops may have a small commitment fee to cover venue and materials, but we offer sponsorships for anyone unable to pay.

Where can I buy the book?+

You can order "Parenting With a Purpose" directly through our contact form or by visiting our office in Pinetown. We also ship across South Africa.

Do you offer 1-on-1 counseling?+

We provide parenting guidance and support. For clinical psychological counseling, we have a network of partners we can refer you to.

How can I volunteer?+

We are always looking for passionate individuals to help with event coordination, administration, and community outreach. Contact us to find out more!
